My true passion lies in good design. I have self-studied UX/UI design, front end development and graphic design for over 3 years.

From the moment I delved into web design, CSS fascinated me. This led to an avalanche of curiosity, prompting questions like "Why do we position elements in specific ways?" and "How can we enhance interactions and usability?" It was during this exploration that I discovered the growing world of UX/UI design, and I am hooked. I love ideating design concepts within a team and translating constraints and ideas into beautiful, and easily accessible designs for end users. I have a persistent hunger for knowledge, and can provide inspiration, enthusiasm, and reliability within any team.
