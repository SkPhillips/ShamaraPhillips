Ro's Bagels Ecommerce Website

For this project I used a random project generator provided by https://randomprojectgenerator.com. It generated a small project brief which included the type of company who needs a website and their ideal customer. This project grew as I went along. The goal of this project was to stretch my skills by creating an awesome responsive website regardless of the type of company or ideal clientele.

DESIGN PROCESS

I began this project by first evaluating the limited, brief description, then putting an appropriate face to the user persona, which played an important role in the initial brainstorming. I continued by executing a stylized presence of the generated mock company "Ro's Bagels". After researching bagel, bakery, and donut shop sites, I gathered ideas for the overall design and feel based on the prompt's keywords: local and handcrafted. Eventually settling on "Ro's Artisan Bagels", I put together a moodboard reflecting clean and warm tones, which served as a basis for building a wireframe with robust components and concept animations. To make the mock company more believable, I sketched out a few logo concepts as well as custom icons that would be used throughout the site. Finally, I designed the high-fidelity prototype and fine-tuned the animations.

WEBSITE ANIMATIONS

This project was created entirely in Adobe XD. There are several micro animations within the mobile and desktop website. One of them to highlight is the checkout confirmation animation of a delivery truck. As well as one of my favorites within the mobile version is an animation of a bagel landing into the cart once "add to cart" is selected.

CLOSING

This project proved to be incredibly captivating. From the initial draft to the testing of animations, it demonstrated how a simple concept can foster boundless creativity and flourish.
